AlturaTime: Global Smart Class Schedule Sharing

What is AlturaTime?
AlturaTime is a global platform for students and families. It lets students upload their class schedules (from any university or school, worldwide) and share a smart, live link with parents, friends, and anyone.

Why? Because families want to call, but never know if it's a good time! AlturaTime solves this: your loved ones always see your local student time, if you're in class, and when your next class starts—no matter where they are.

For: Families with students studying far from home. Parents and friends wanting to stay close. Students who want to be less interrupted and more connected.

How does it work?
  • Upload your class schedule .ics file (from Google/Apple Calendar, university portal, Outlook, etc.)
  • Enter your name and submit.
  • Get a secure, unique, shareable link.
  • Your family sees live status: your local time, if you're in class, and a countdown to the next class.
  • Works anywhere in the world. 100% timezone-accurate.

Built for a million+ families and students worldwide. Zero setup. Zero confusion. Only connection.

Your schedule is stored securely and only accessible via your generated link.

How do I get my .ics file?
- From Google Calendar: Settings → "Export", or open the event → "Export as .ics".
- From Apple Calendar: File → Export → Export...
- From most university portals: Look for "Download Calendar" or "Export .ics".
- From Outlook: File → Save As → iCalendar format.

Is my data safe?
Yes. Your schedule is securely stored and only accessible with your unique link. We do not track, sell, or share your data.

Need help?
If your link does not work or you have trouble with your .ics file, contact your academic IT or try re-exporting your schedule.
